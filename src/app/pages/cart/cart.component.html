About The American University

The American University (AU) is a prestigious private research university located in Washington, D.C. Founded in 1893, it has a strong commitment to academic excellence, social justice, and global impact. AU offers a broad range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional degree programs, with an emphasis on interdisciplinary education and practical experience. The university is known for its strong programs in international relations, political science, law, business, public policy, and communication, as well as its focus on liberal arts education. AU provides students with a vibrant campus life, including over 150 student organizations, leadership opportunities, and a commitment to service and community engagement. With a diverse student body representing all 50 states and over 140 countries, AU fosters a global perspective, making it a dynamic hub for intellectual growth and cultural exchange.
